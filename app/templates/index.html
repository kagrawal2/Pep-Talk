What is PepTalk?

PepTalk is a card style application designed to add encouragement for your longterm goals and resolutions.

Using your goal and description we suggest multimedia like videos from YouTube, encouraging and relevant quotes, and music, PepTalk reminds you to get your goals done in a more stimulating and encouraging way.

For a test trial we also offer an Anonymous Demo mode, in which your data will be deleted after a week of inactivity.
