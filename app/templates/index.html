What is PepTalk?

PepTalk is a card-style application designed to add encouragement to your longterm goals and resolutions.

  • By using your goal title and description we suggest multimedia like videos from YouTube & encouraging quotes, PepTalk reminds you to achieve your goals in a more stimulating and motivating way.
  • For a test trial, we also offer an Anonymous Demo mode, in which your data will be deleted after a week of inactivity.
